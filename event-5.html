Description

There are many environments being used for the developement of of VR & AR but two of them stand out for their ubiquity and ease of use. We will look at Unity3D and Unreal Engine and compare and contrast their use cases for different projects. We will also look at the basic structure of game engines and how to use them for VR & AR development.
